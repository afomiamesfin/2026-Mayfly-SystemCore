These are the acronyms / names to use for 
Choreo files & naming autos

Note: left & right are considered from the driverstation POV
 so, the depot is to the LEFT & outpost to the RIGHT

LB & RB = left & right bump
LT & RT = left & right trench
LN & RN = left & right neutral zone
LC & RC = left & right climb
S = score
H = hub (ex. stopping in front of it)
D = depot
O = outpost
